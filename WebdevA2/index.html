Fighter Jets

What do we know about them? Let this website help you discover facts about fighter jets that you may not know about!

Speed

Most well known fact about fighter jets are their speed. They average speeds of 2 Mach, or 2,500 km/h at minimum. Thats 2 times the speed of sound!

The fastest fighter jet that is still in service today is the Mikoyan-Gurevich MiG-25 Foxbat. It can reach speeds of up to 3.2 Mach, or 4,000 km/h.

How high can you go?

The tallest object in the world is Mount Everest, at 8,848m.
Commercial planes fly at a height of about 10,668m.
Jet fighters fly at a height of about 14,630m
